Here is a quick timeline of Rupaul's life:

  • 1960: Rupaul was born on November 17, in San Diego, California.
  • 1987: Rupaul helps create Starbooty , a low budget film starring then-unknown drag queens, such as Lady Bunny.
  • 1993: Rupaul releases Supermodel of the World. The album's hit track, Supermodel of the World (You Better Work) becomes a major success, peaking at #45 on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • 1996: Rupaul's debuts The Rupaul Show, along with his co-host Michelle Visage. The show would eventually run for 100 episodes before ultimately being cancelled.
  • 2007: Rupaul releases Starrbooty (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack). The film was released in October 2007.
  • 2008: Rupaul debuts Rupaul's Drag Race on LOGO, a drag queen competition to find "America's next Drag Superstar". The show has spawned 8 main seasons, 2 All-Star seasons, and has been a major success.
  • 2013: Rupaul joins forces with Colorevolution and debuts his makeup line alongside with his unisex perfume, Glamazon.
  • 2016: Rupaul debuts his game show, Gay For Play, on LOGO.
